The Best Movies According to Akrem

My top 3 movies of all time

SISU

"Sisu" is a 2022 Finnish action film set during the Lapland War towards the end of World War II.The story follows Aatami Korpi, a former commando turned gold prospector, who discovers gold in the Lapland wilderness. As he attempts to take his treasure into the city, he encounters a retreating Nazi platoon led by a brutal SS officer. The Nazis, intent on seizing his gold, soon realize they've engaged with no ordinary miner. Aatami embodies 'sisu,' a Finnish term representing extraordinary determination and courage in the face of overwhelming odds.

The Great Wall

"The Great Wall" is a 2016 action-fantasy film directed by Zhang Yimou. The story follows European mercenaries William (Matt Damon) and Tovar (Pedro Pascal) who, while searching for gunpowder in China, are captured and imprisoned within the Great Wall. They soon discover that the Wall is defended by a secret army dedicated to protecting against monstrous creatures known as the Tao Tei. As the creatures launch a massive assault, William and Tovar join forces with the Chinese warriors to combat the relentless horde.

Nobody

"Nobody" is a 2021 action thriller directed by Ilya Naishuller. The film stars Bob Odenkirk as Hutch Mansell, a seemingly ordinary family man who, after a home invasion, reverts to his former life as an assassin to protect his family from a vengeful crime lord. The movie explores themes of identity and suppressed violence, delivering intense action sequences and a darkly comedic narrative.
